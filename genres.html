Explore Book Genres

Fiction

Fiction books create imaginative stories, often in a variety of subgenres like historical, fantasy, and more.

Nonfiction

Nonfiction books are based on factual information, covering topics like biography, history, and more.

Poetry

Poetry is a form of literary expression using rhythmic and often metaphorical language.

Fantasy

Fantasy books contain magical elements, often set in imaginary worlds with mystical creatures and heroes.

Romance

Romance books explore relationships and love, often with an emotional, heartfelt storyline.

Mystery

Mystery books center around solving a crime, puzzle, or an enigma, often with a detective or investigator.
